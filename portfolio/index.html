COVID-19 Crisis Operations — Station-Wide Accountability from Scratch

2020

Air Force Station Palam. COVID lockdown. No digital tracking existed for station-wide manpower accountability. 500+ personnel dispersed across units. Simultaneously responsible for four departments' operational continuity.

Diagnosed the accountability gap: no system existed to track personnel across dispersed units in real time. Designed and built an attendance and accountability system providing real-time personnel status visibility across all station units — iterating in rapid build-test-deploy cycles — logic designed one day, live the next. Managed documentation, equipment readiness, and compliance for four departments simultaneously.

Zero mission downtime across the entire COVID operational period. System adopted as station standard. Daily utilisation by Chief Engineering Officer. Adapted for post-COVID operational efficiency.

Mail Follow-up System — Logic Absorbed into Enterprise ERP

2015

Serving as PA to Air Officer Commanding. Correspondence tracking across the unit was manual — letters lost, follow-ups missed, no aging visibility. Accountability gaps affected operational decisions.

Built a digital correspondence and task tracking system with automated aging alerts. Designed it for 100+ officers across the organisation. Documented the system so it could operate without its creator.

System logic absorbed into IAF E-Office ERP rollout in 2022 — now serving 10,000+ users organisation-wide. 7-year lifecycle from individual initiative to enterprise adoption. Demonstrates sustainable system design.
